Hierachical Orchestration of End-to-End Networks  

High-level Description:

The hierarchical orchestration of E2E networks (HOEN), is a modular and scalable framework for the orchestration and management of end-to-end networks, composed of multiple network segments, e.g. wired and wireless networks. 

 

More Information

For more information about HOEN, please refer to the ORCA deliverable D4.3.
